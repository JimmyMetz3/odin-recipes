Steak

Description

A simple, yet elegant dish, the steak. The most controversial topic being the color or temperature at which the steak is served.

Ingredients

  • Steak (Just pick your favorite cut)
  • Butter
  • Minced garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season your steak with salt and pepper heavily(Some seasoning will come off during the cooking process)
  2. Fire up your grill
  3. Heat up a bowl of butter with minced garlic
  4. Place steak on the grill and flip when needed
    • Doneness is based on preference. I take my steak off when the internal temperature is 135 degress farenheit.
    • While flipping the steak, be sure to lather your steak with your garlic butter
  5. Take steak off heat and let it rest for a minimum of 7 minutes
  6. Lastly, enjoy your steak with your loved ones!
