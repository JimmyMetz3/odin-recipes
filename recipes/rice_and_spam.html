Rice & Spam

Decription

My go to dish since I was a small child. Simple, easy, and filling!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Spam
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse your rice at least two times
  2. Put rice into your rice cooker
  3. Place your index finger on the surface of the rice
  4. Fill with water until the water reaches the middle of your first knuckle
  5. Power on rice cooker and let it perform it's magic
  6. Cut your spam into slices, size is up to you. I like mine with a thickness of a quarter of an inch.
  7. Cook your spam in a non-stick skillet to desired crispiness
  8. Enjoy!!
