Chili

Description

A delish and versatile dish. You can make it spicy or sweeter based on preference. Enjoy it as an appetizer or as an entree!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Yellow onion
  • Red bell pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomato sauce
  • Kidney beans
  • Pinto beans
  • Worcestershire sauce(Do not ask me to say that)
  • Beef broth
  • Hot sauce
  • Chili powder
  • Ground Cumin
  • Smoke paprika
  • Cayenne
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Brown beef and italian sausage
  2. After the meat is mostly brown, some pink is fine, add diced onions and bell pepper. Cook over medium heat until the onion is translucent
  3. Add your beans
  4. Add the rest of your Ingredients
  5. Gently stir until all ingredients have been throughly mixed in
  6. Let the pot sit on a low temperature for 4 hours. Stir occasionally.
